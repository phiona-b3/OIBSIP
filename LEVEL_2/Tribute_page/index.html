Oladiwura Oladepo

Executive Director

@Tech4Dev

”I wanted to be an entrepreneur because it would allow me to make critical decisions, implement them, and more importantly, to make a difference.”

Oladiwura is the Executive Director at Technology for Social Change and Development Initiative (Tech4Dev), a Nigerian based Non-Profit Organization which gives access to decent work for Africans. Oladepo and Joel Ogunsola started Tech4Dev in 2014 during the Ebola crisis in their country. The idea was to provide relevant information about the disease in a timely manner.

Their informational website ultimately received over one million unique visits. She is the Chief Growth Officer at Prunedge, a technology solutions company in Lagos. Her contributions and goal of ending poverty in Africa and around the world have not gone unnoticed.
